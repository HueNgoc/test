Importance

Protecting wildlife is crucial for maintaining biodiversity and ecosystem balance. It also provides significant economic benefits through ecotourism and supports cultural and spiritual values. Additionally, conserving wildlife helps prevent the spread of diseases from animals to humans. Overall, wildlife protection is essential for environmental health and human well-being.
